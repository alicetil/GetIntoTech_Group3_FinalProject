City Centre


Osterley doesn't exactly have London city centre on its doorstep but it might be easier than you think to make a trip into the Big Smoke. A direct train from Syon Lane (a short walk from Osterley campus) takes you to Putney, Waterloo and London Bridge in under an hour. Perhaps on your next visit to Osterley campus, you'd like to try one of these recommendations to enjoy an evening in London itself.
